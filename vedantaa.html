At Vedanta, we are focused on nation building through sustainable growth while setting high standards of corporate governance and transparency. At our core is a mission to boost manufacturing, reduce imports and make India ‘atmanirbhar’ in natural resources. Over the years, we have raised $35 billion overseas and invested into the natural resources sector in the country. Our journey has been synonymous with the India growth story and we have contributed ₹2,740 billion to the exchequer in the last 10 years. We account for ~1.4 per cent of India’s GDP.
