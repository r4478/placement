Skills /Eligibility

  • Responsibilities and Duties:


    1. Provide technology consulting services to law firms and corporates

    2. Understand underlying technologies to analyze patent portfolios and help solve clients’ core technical queries

    3. Conduct research & analysis, including but not limited to technology due diligence, market research, prior art searches, target scouting, invalidity analysis, infringement analysis and claim charting

    4. Be a part of the product development team in developing the legal tech product.

    5. Work in different aspects of the product life-cycle

    6. Explain complex technology matters to non-technical audiences

    7. Excavate evidence to identify patent infringement using product testing, source code review and product documentation research

    8. Build models to analyze large data and draw insights Work with product teams to guide development & testing of Cipher’s technology platforms

    9. Showcase technology and IP thought leadership via blogs and research reports

    10. Collaborate across the organization in-person and with virtual, global teams

    11. Establish and cultivate relationships internally and externally

    Expected Competencies | Skills:


    1. Strong fundamentals in telecom, software architecture, high-scale computing, computer security, cryptography, databases, and/or applied mathematics

    2. Familiarity with products, services, and standards in web, mobile, enterprise, media, etc.

    3. Excellent verbal and written communication skills, especially in technical writing

    4. Proficiency in technical research.

    5. Ability to think critically, learn fast and work with minimal supervision under tight deadlines.

    6. Entrepreneurial and go-getter attitude.
