Virohan is a Healthcare focused Ed-Tech company training youth for jobs in the healthcare sector. We aim to create India’s largest healthcare vocational company with industry demand-driven curriculum. We are funded by Elea Foundation for Ethics in Globalization, Artha Impact, Yunus Social Business (an impact fund founded by Noble Laureate Prof. Mohammad Yunus), AngelList, Keiretsu Forum amongst others. Workforce readiness is a huge problem in India. There is a huge disconnect between demand and supply of the skills in the workforce across sectors forcing youth to compromise on aspirations, income and location. Virohan uses technology and talent to fix this socio-economic problem, so that industry demand and skilled workforce are in perfect equilibrium. With a team of 130+ and training centres across 20 cities, Virohan is scaling up and looking for fast, focused and flexible talent with great problem-solving abilities who want to also make the world a better place.
