Skills /Eligibility

  • Responsibilities :


    • Analysis of datasets to generate descriptive statistics and propose potential applications of data

    • Developing new applications to assist traders in efficiently operating and monitoring trading strategies

    • Working closely with quantitative traders in Bangalore to develop analytics, analyze market data sets, and troubleshoot trading issues.

    • Writing highly latency-sensitive and scalable code that will process large amounts of data.

    • Developing systems for analyzing and visualizing huge datasets

    Qualifications :


    • Excellent knowledge of C++ and python

    • Excellent analytical skills and very good attention to detail

    • Good understanding of Object Oriented Programming concepts and C++

    • Knowledge of shell scripting is a plus

    • Experience with open source visualization tools is a plus
